About

This map application was designed to serve Calgary’s residents by providing key public services data combined with essential environmental and social information. This platform offers a comprehensive view of the city’s infrastructure, safety, and environmental priorities, helping you make informed decisions that impact daily life and future sustainability.

Analysis

The Calgary Community Map seamlessly integrates a wide array of data sources, empowering users to thoroughly explore and analyze critical aspects of urban life. By combining various datasets, this innovative platform offers valuable insights into the city's infrastructure, safety, and community services, fostering a deeper understanding of the environment in which residents live. Below, we highlight the key features of our application that enhance user experience and promote informed decision-making.

Crime Heatmap

Visualize areas with higher crime rates in Calgary using historical data from Calgary Police Services to assess safety effectively.

Police Stations

Locate police stations in Calgary, complete with contact info and hours for quick access to law enforcement.

Community Services

Access vital public services like health clinics, recreation centers, and libraries to enhance community quality of life.

Traffic Flow

Stay informed with real-time traffic updates with detailed traffic volume data so you can plan your commute effectively.

Priority Species in Canada

Discover Canada's priority species needing conservation to promote environmental awareness and community action.
